Blog #1: Picking A Banner Image

May 14, 2020

As I am currently in the process of building this site, I want to reflect on one very important decision I made, and that is the choice of image for my banner. After scrolling through a ton of photos to find the perfect banner, I realised I have been wasting unneccessary time and energy into evaluating each option. I did not know what I wanted in my banner, which led to unconcentrated efforts in finding one. Therefore, I sat down and listed two requirements for the banner:
    1. It does not take away from the main focus of the text
    2. It is pleasant to look at
These were very simple requirements, and as a result, I was able to quickly pick one that fits the criteria.

This is why having something concrete and realistic to aim for allows for better productivity. According to Parkinson's Law, "work expands to fill the avaliable time for its completion", and in my opinion, apart from procrastination, the cause for this phenomenon is the perfectionist in all of us.
